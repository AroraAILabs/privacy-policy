1. Introduction

Welcome to Reminders ("the App"), developed by Arora AI Labs ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

This policy applies to the Reminders mobile application available on the Google Play Store and any associated services. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Minimization & Offline-First Design

The Reminders app is built with privacy by design. We minimize data collection and processing:

  • No backend servers: The app has no custom backend infrastructure. There is no server to deploy or cloud database.
  • No data transmission: Your reminder data is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database (Room).
  • No analytics, tracking, or ads: We do not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or employ third-party analytics services.
  • No third-party data sharing: We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

Core Principle: All core reminder functionality (create, edit, delete, view, search, notifications, recurring reminders, widget) works completely offline without requiring internet connectivity or account sign-in.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to external servers:

  • Reminder Content: Title, description, category, due date/time, priority, recurrence rules, tags, attachments
  • Reminder Status: Completion status, archive status, snooze settings
  • User Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, language selection, app UI customization (all stored in EncryptedSharedPreferences)

3.2 Optional Google Account Data

If you choose to use optional features (email delivery or cloud backup), the following applies:

  • Google OAuth Sign-In: Reminders supports optional Google OAuth authentication only to enable email delivery (via Gmail API) and cloud backup (via Google Drive API).
  • Email Delivery: If you enable email notifications, we access your Google account solely to deliver reminders via your Gmail account. Your OAuth token is stored securely on your device using EncryptedSharedPreferences.
  • Cloud Backup: If you enable cloud backup, your reminder data may be backed up to your personal Google Drive account as a JSON file. You retain full control and can disable backups at any time.
  • No direct data collection: We do not directly store your Google account credentials, email address, or any personal identifiers on our servers.

3.3 Device Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions for functionality:

  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Required to schedule precise reminders
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Required to reschedule reminders after device reboot
  • INTERNET — Required only if you enable email delivery or cloud backup features
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Required to display notification reminders on Android 13+

All permissions serve only the stated functionality and are not used for any other purpose.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for the functionality you request:

  • Reminder Management: To store, retrieve, and display your reminders within the app
  • Notifications: To trigger and deliver push notifications at scheduled times using Android's AlarmManager (all processing occurs locally on your device)
  • Email Delivery (optional): If enabled, to send reminder notifications via your Gmail account using the Gmail API
  • Cloud Backup (optional): If enabled, to securely back up your reminder data to your personal Google Drive account
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs and improve app stability based on your explicit user feedback (if provided)

We do not use your data for:

  • Marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes
  • Profiling, behavioral tracking, or analytics
  • Building user segments or demographic analysis
  • Sharing with affiliates, partners, or third parties

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage Security

Your reminder data and preferences are protected using industry-standard encryption:

  • Database Encryption: SQLite database is encrypted using SQLCipher when data at rest encryption is enabled on the device
  • Credential Storage: Google OAuth tokens and sensitive preferences are encrypted using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • Device-Level Protection: We rely on Android's built-in security framework (SELinux, sandbox isolation, permission system)

5.2 No Third-Party Backend Servers

Reminders has no custom backend infrastructure. No data is transmitted to or stored on servers controlled by Arora AI Labs or its partners, except:

  • Google APIs only: Gmail and Google Drive APIs are called directly from your device using your OAuth token. Data flows from your device → your Google account, not through our infrastructure.

5.3 Data Retention

Data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can:

  • Delete individual reminders within the app at any time
  • Clear all data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable cloud backup to prevent future backups to Google Drive

6. Third-Party Services

The Reminders app integrates with the following optional third-party services:

6.1 Google APIs

  • Gmail API: Used only when you explicitly enable email delivery for reminders. Calls are made directly from your device using your OAuth token. No data is stored on Arora AI Labs servers.
  • Google Drive API: Used only when you explicitly enable cloud backup. Backup files are stored in your personal Google Drive account.
  • Google OAuth: Used only for authentication and sign-in. We do not store your Google credentials.

For details on how Google handles your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

6.2 No Other Third-Party Integrations

The app does not integrate with:

  • Third-party analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Crash reporting or error tracking services
  • Advertising networks or ad exchanges
  • Social media platforms
  • Marketing or email campaign services

7. Children's Privacy

The Reminders app is not targeted at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

For children 13 and older, parental consent is not required, but we recommend that parents supervise their use of the app.

Compliance with applicable children's privacy laws (such as COPPA in the United States) is taken seriously. If you are aware of any violations, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can access all your reminder data stored on your device at any time within the app.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete individual reminders or all data by uninstalling the app.
  • Right to Portability: You can export your reminder data as JSON files using the app's export feature.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can revoke Google API access at any time through your Google Account settings or the app settings.

Since we do not maintain centralized servers storing your data, data access and deletion requests are self-service within the app.

9. Data Breach Notification

Since the Reminders app stores all data locally on your device with no centralized backend servers, the risk of a data breach affecting our systems is eliminated.

However, if any security vulnerability is discovered in the app that could affect your data, we will:

  • Release a security patch as soon as possible
  • Notify users through the Google Play Store update mechanism
  • Provide clear guidance on recommended actions

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The Reminders app does not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any other tracking technologies. Since the app is offline-first and has no backend servers, cookies are not applicable.

11. International Data Transfers

Since all your reminder data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to servers outside of optional Google API calls, international data transfers do not occur, except:

  • If you enable cloud backup to Google Drive, your data is transferred to Google's servers, which operate globally. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
